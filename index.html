ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

  • App crashes or won't open

    Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, restart your device and try again. Make sure you're running the latest version of SnapClean.

  • Photos not loading

    Check that SnapClean has photo permissions enabled. Go to your device settings and ensure photo access is granted. Also verify that you have photos in your gallery.

  • Can't delete photos

    Ensure SnapClean has permission to modify photos. On iOS, this is included with photo access. On Android, you may need to grant additional storage permissions.

  • App shows "No photos found"

    This usually means either SnapClean doesn't have photo permissions, or your photo library is empty. Check permissions and verify you have photos in your device's gallery.

  • Performance issues

    If the app is slow or unresponsive, try closing other apps, clearing your device's cache, or restarting your device. Large photo libraries may take longer to load initially.
