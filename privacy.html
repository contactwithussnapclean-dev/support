Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

SnapClean respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Data Processing

  • SnapClean processes your photos locally on your device.
  • We do not upload, store, or share your photos on any server.

2. Data We Collect

  • We do not collect personal information, photos, or gallery data.
  • The app may store basic settings/preferences on your device only.

3. Third-Party Services

  • SnapClean does not integrate advertising or analytics SDKs that collect personal data.

4. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at:

Son güncelleme: Aralık 2024

1. Veri İşleme

  • SnapClean, kişisel veri toplayan reklam veya analiz SDK'ları içermez.
